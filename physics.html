Physics

We’re learning the Advanced Physics course, a challenging yet very gratifying physics course. We’re learning the foundations of physics and how we can extrapolate them to more and more complicated real-world applications. What I really like about physics is that it isn’t an abstract science—you can concretely see the applications and physical connotations of each concept we learn. It’s an amazing course, and I hope to pursue physics further in the future. .
