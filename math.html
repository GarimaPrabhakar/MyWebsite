Mathematics

In math, we’ve been exploring Linear Transformations. We’re not looking just at the cookie-cutter linear transformations for points that you’ll learn in any middle school math class, but instead looking at how it is possible to redefine the very geometrical space you’re mapping your point on with math. I realized that the only way to really appreciate the power and breadth of the linear transformation topic is to delve deeper into it—it is a vast and incredible cool subject to learn once you get to know it.

HiMCM Competition

One of my favorite parts of the math class was the HiMCM (High School Math Contest in Modelling). We got into groups and participated in a 14-day math modeling competition. It was one of the most memorable and gratifying experiences at MAMS. The goal was to analyze free public energy consumption across the United States. Our team developed a simulation algorithm to do so using various realistic scenarios. Below is our 19 page HiMCM paper.
