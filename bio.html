About Me

I live in a family of five, with my mother, father, seven year old brother, and my fourteen year old sister! We live in Massachusetts, with about 100,000 bees for neighbors on our left. Even though I grew up here, I was born in Los Angeles, California. People say it takes a village to rear a child, and I feel that was true for me in the fullest sense. I grew up in the midst of more of a village of family friends-- aunties and uncles and friends of friends, so throughout my life I've had a giant family of support that have all brought me up and made me who I am.

Last year, my family and I went to Egypt for vacation, and it was amazing! It was definitely and awesome break from the daily routine, and we got to visit so many amazing places. We saw the Great Pyramids and the Red Sea, and even King Tutankhamun's mummy. On the picture on the right, My father, mother, my brother and sister, and me are standing in a hall of one of Egypt's ancient temples.

Right is the other part of my family: the four or five families in Massachusetts that are part of our socio-spiritual organization: Ananda Marga. We gather every Sunday for DC at one of our houses, and afterwards we have a (predictably delicious) lunch! I've known these people for most of my life, and everyone has impacted my life in a major way. I'm so grateful that I had everyone at Boston's DC group for my life, and I can't wait to give back.
