Spanish

We’re doing an immersive Advanced Spanish course in which we experience as much Spanish as we can in and out of class. Moreover, our Spanish class is one of the most fun and engaging Spanish classes I’ve taken—we approach learning Spanish in a variety of angles which not only build up our proficiency in every component of a language but also make the process incredibly fun.

One of the most interesting and fun parts of Spanish was the grade-wide Language meme contest. In the contest, we submitted individually or in groups a meme in the language we were learning about either the class, our school, or the world in general. Our teacher posted the memes on the board and we got to share the memes amongst ourselves. It was a really fun time coming up with memes and seeing other students’ memes. To the left is my meme.
